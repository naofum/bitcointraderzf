Place Order

This view allows you to create a new order. You can choose between "ask" orders (sell bitcoins) and "bit" orders (buy bitcoins).

By checking the "marketorder" box, you create an order, which is executed at the current market price.

As an alternative, you can choose the price and amount you want to trade. To autofill the current price, click the currency_pair next to the price-field. If you want to sell all your bitcoins, click the currency_pair next to the amount-field.
